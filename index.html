📊 MARKET INTELLIGENCE SUMMARY
UAE Advantage: UAE delivers the only combination of strong YoY growth (+20–32%), low bubble risk, zero capital gains tax, and demand-led appreciation globally. Abu Dhabi and RAK offer value vs Dubai with steeper growth curves.
Watch Points: Dubai's 120,000-unit 2026 pipeline may moderate prices 1–8%. Off-plan dominance (68–85% of deals) creates delivery risk. Monitor interest rates for mortgage-financed buyers (though UAE is predominantly cash).
Global Context: London, Toronto, and Hong Kong in correction/stagnation (–2% to –8% YoY). UAE remains standout performer driven by 200K+ residents/year inflow and $63B wealth migration recorded in 2025.
